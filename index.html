When you start answering, four chances may occur

1. He/She gets bored.
2. May not understand what you are trying to say?
3. Will reject because you are not answering to the point.
4. He/She may interrupt you and start asking questions.
So always better to prepare in advance.

Explaining the whole project effectively in just 3-4 minutes is the difficult task.

How do we do that ??

Go step wise →
1. Name of your project and your Domain.
2. Convey the main purpose of your project ( Be simple and clear ).
3. Tell them how your project is built ( What tools, technologies, platform, process are used ).
4. How long is your project going on.
5. Your role

This is how we are trying to explain all the areas that we are aware of and not getting deep into content.
